Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2025

YouTube Playlist Sorter (“the App”) uses the YouTube Data API to retrieve and display your playlists and videos. By using the App you agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

  • Google OAuth tokens: Used only in your current browser session to call the YouTube Data API. We do not send these tokens to any server we control, and we do not store them after the session ends.
  • Local storage (on your device): We store lightweight preferences such as theme/low-power mode, favorites, and your personal view counters via your browser’s localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to sync/backup your browser profile.

How We Use Information

  • To list and sort your YouTube playlists/videos you select.
  • To remember local preferences and counters via localStorage.
  • If enabled, to display advertisements (see “Ads & Cookies” below).

Third-Party Services

  • Google APIs (OAuth 2.0, YouTube Data API)
  • Google AdSense (for displaying advertisements, if enabled)

These services may process data in accordance with their own policies. Please review the Google Privacy Policy and the YouTube Terms of Service.

Ads & Cookies (AdSense)

If ads are enabled on your version of the App: third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and/or other websites. Google’s advertising cookies enable Google and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

  • Manage your ad personalization in Google Ads Settings.
  • Learn more about how Google uses data in ads: About Google Ads.
  • Some partners may also set cookies. Your browser settings and (where applicable) a consent banner let you control these choices.

Aside from AdSense (if enabled), the App itself does not set tracking cookies. It uses only localStorage for on-device preferences.

Your Choices & Data Deletion

  • Revoke access: You can revoke the App’s access to your Google account at any time via Google Account → Security → Third-party access.
  • Clear local data: Use the in-app “Reset Personal Views”/logout, or clear your browser’s site data to remove locally stored preferences.
  • Logout/Switch accounts: Use the in-app logout to end the session and return to the sign-in screen.

EEA/UK Users (Legal Bases)

Where applicable under GDPR/UK GDPR: OAuth tokens are processed on the basis of performance of a contract (to provide requested features). Ad personalization (if enabled) is processed on the basis of consent via a consent banner. Non-personalized ads may be shown where required.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has used the App, please contact us for guidance on removing local data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.
