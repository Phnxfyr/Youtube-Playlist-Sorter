Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2025

This application uses the YouTube Data API to retrieve and display user playlist information. By using this application, you agree to be bound by the YouTube Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

We do not store any personal information on our servers. Any access tokens retrieved from your Google account are used only for the current session and are not saved or transmitted elsewhere. Your personal view counts are saved locally on your device through browser localStorage and are not transmitted or stored externally.

Third-Party Services

This app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google APIs (OAuth, YouTube Data API)
  • Google AdSense (for displaying relevant advertisements)

These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies. Please review those policies to understand how your information is handled.

Cookies and Tracking

This app does not use cookies or track users outside of the localStorage data required for user functionality like saving theme preferences or view counts.

Your Choices

You can clear personal views or logout/switch accounts using the settings menu in the app. Clearing your browser data will also remove stored information.
